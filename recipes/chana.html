Chana recipe

What is Chana?

Chana is a popular Indian dish made with chickpeas in an onion tomato gravy. It goes great as a side with rice, paratha or roti.

Cooking time: 30 mins

Serves 6

Ingredients:

  • Two Cans of Chickpeas
  • One small onion
  • Half a potato
  • Two tomatoes
  • Mixed powder
  • Salt
  • Corriander (Optional)

Method

  1. Place and grease a pan onto medium heat
  2. Finely dice your onion and add it to your pan
  3. While they brown peel and dice your potato
  4. Add it to your pan and cook until relatively soft
  5. Add 4 tablepsoons of mixed powder and half a tablepsoon of salt
  6. Add your rouphly choppd tomatoes
  7. Add a cup of water and leave it to simmer for 15-20 mins
  8. Add coriander and enjoy!
