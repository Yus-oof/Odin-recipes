Rice pudding recipe

What is rice pudding?

Rice pudding is a dish made from rice mixed with water or milk and other ingredients such as cinnamon, vanilla and raisins.

Cooking time: 55 mins

Serves 4

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • White rice
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon optional

Method:

  1. Add five and a half cups of milk with half a cup of sugar and a pinch of salt
  2. Boil these over medium-high heat
  3. Stir in half a cup of rice on low heat
  4. Cook until a yoghurt-like consistency is reached
  5. Remove from heat and add vanilla
  6. Let cool and refrigerate
  7. Stir in half a cup of milk before serving
  8. Add cinnamon and enjoy!
