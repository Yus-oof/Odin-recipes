Roti Recipe

What is Roti?

Roti or Chapati is everyday Indian flatbread which is made with only 2 ingredients- whole wheat flour (atta) and water.

Cooking time: 1 hour

Makes 6

Ingredients:

  • Self rasing flour
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Water

Method:

  1. Add 225g of flour to a bowl
  2. Add oil, salt and water and mix to form a dough
  3. Knead until smooth
  4. Cover and let it rest for 30 mins
  5. Flour a surface and roll out balls of your dough
  6. Oil a pan and cook your roti until it puffs
  7. Serve with your chana and enjoy!
